Chlorinating Ltd, has been in existence since 1978. We have established ourselves as a leading contractor in the field of water disinfection of what will be the potable water source. Today chlorination is the most popular method of disinfection and is used for water treatment all over the world. Chlorinating Ltd has over 35 years of experience in sterilization of water facilities all over the Chicago land area. We have a respected working relationship with engineering firms as well as local municipalities. We are a proud member of the Underground Contractors Association (UCA) and the American Water Works Association ( AWWA). Our interaction with these groups gives us up to date information on the latest EPA standards, rules and regulations so as to provide the safest professional work environment in the industry. Chlorinating Ltd has a professional and responsible management team. Our work force is dedicated to meeting and exceeding the expectations of our clients.
